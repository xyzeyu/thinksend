Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

ThinkSend is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app and website.

1. Local Data

Your notes are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit your notes to our servers.

2. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (with your consent) to understand how our website is used. You can decline analytics cookies at any time.

3. Pro Features

Pro features that integrate with external apps (like Google, WhatsApp, or Trello) will open those apps in your browser. ThinkSend does not store or collect your login credentials.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at thinksendev@gmail.com.
